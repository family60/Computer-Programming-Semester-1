Welcome to the movie summary of Joker

This movie is about a man named Arthur. The audience not just sees but experiences what it is like to live a poor life in Gotham City. Arthur starts off as a confused and depressed, mentally ill person. As the film goes on, the character soon wake up to his own reality, that he didn't want to behave how others wanted him to but instead, how he wanted to. He continues to act out in various ways, mostly consisting of killing everyone close to him. At the end of the film, Arthur - now known as "Joker" - kills late night talk show host Murray. This triggers an outbreak in Gotham as every poor person begins to burn the city down and commit crimes out of rebellion for the rich. As the city burns, the Joker is crowned as the leader for the cult and all law and order is destroyed.
